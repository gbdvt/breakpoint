# Breakpoint

## One-sentence pitch

Breakpoint detects when cognitive overload turns into avoidance behaviors (tab spirals, compulsive checking, delayed task starts) and helps users recover before patterns compound into burnout.

## Target users

- Students
- Engineers and on-call staff
- Knowledge workers

## Core problem

Users do not only “get distracted.” Common loops include:

1. **Reactive drift** — reflex opens of feeds and distractors (YouTube, LinkedIn, etc.).
2. **Research inflation** — adding tutorials, docs, and videos before execution (“prep” that replaces doing).
3. **Task-start friction** — long delay between session start and a first meaningful action.

These behaviors often feel productive, stay invisible in the moment, and add stress over time.

## What Breakpoint does

- Detects behavioral patterns in near real time (extension in a later phase; dashboard MVP uses simulated events).
- Offers light, non-shaming interventions at high-signal moments.
- Surfaces hidden time cost (e.g. research queue estimates as a stretch goal).
- Provides a session debrief: timeline, first drift, and simple sequence.

## What Breakpoint is not

- Not therapy or clinical care.
- Not a diagnostic tool for ADHD, burnout, or any condition.
- Not a generic “block everything” productivity enforcer.
- Not a claim to read intent or “truth” of relevance without transparent, optional AI assistance later.
